The Lore of Crimson Night

A Kingdom Hidden from Dawn

Long before cities touched the sky, there stood a kingdom wrapped in black stone and red mist. Its people feared the sun, worshipped the moon, and guarded a secret older than memory itself.

The Crimson Covenant was formed by seven vampire houses, each sworn to protect the castle, the forest, and the sacred bloodline of the first immortal.
